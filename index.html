The idea of a detergant bottle is extremely simple, yet difficult to design using SolidWorks. Using surfacing commands such as boundary surface and filled surface paired with 3D sketches, I was able to create a bottle with close to perfect curvature.

Detergant Bottle



Creating a detergant bottle involvs heavy use of surfacing commands such as boundary surface, surface extrude, and fill surfaces. Due to complex geometry, a combination of good curvature and tangencies between edges was time consuming to achieve. This bottle is designed for injection molding.

Skills used:

  • SolidWorks
  • Design for Plastics
